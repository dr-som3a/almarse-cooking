what we do?

Moroccan Tajine

Slow-cooked in a clay pot with preserved lemon, olives, vegetables, and fragrant spices — a symbol of Moroccan hospitality.

Vegetarian Couscous

Steamed semolina grains served with seasonal vegetables and chickpeas, delicately spiced for a wholesome Berber delight.

Rfisa

A comforting dish of shredded msemen or trid soaked in a spiced chicken and lentil broth — deeply rooted in Moroccan tradition.

Why Choose Almarse Cooking Class?

EXPERIENCE MOROCCO THROUGH FLAVOR

At Almarse Cooking Class, you're not just learning recipes — you're stepping into Moroccan culture. Cook side by side with local chefs, discover secret family techniques, and master traditional dishes like couscous, rfisa, and tajine. It’s more than food — it’s an experience you’ll take home.

Skills You’ll Master in Class

Fruit Plating Ritual

Learn the beauty of Moroccan fruit presentation using fresh dates, oranges, and figs in colorful arrangements passed down through generations.

Berber Bread Craft

Bake khobz the way it's done in Berber villages: by hand, by fire, and by heart. From mixing to the clay oven, it's a ritual you’ll love.

Mint Tea Ceremony

Master the slow pour, the scent of fresh mint, and the hospitality behind every cup of Moroccan tea — a cultural art form in motion.
