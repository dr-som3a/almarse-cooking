Rfisa Ingredients

Explore the layers of Rfisa, a traditional Moroccan dish made from msemen, lentils, chicken, and fenugreek-rich broth.

How to Prepare

  1. Brown chicken in olive oil with garlic and onions.
  2. Add lentils, turmeric, ginger, ras el hanout, saffron.
  3. Pour in stock and simmer until chicken and lentils are tender.
  4. Shred msemen or trid and plate it.
  5. Ladle the stew over the bread and serve hot.
