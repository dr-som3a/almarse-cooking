How Mint Tea Became a Symbol of Moroccan Hospitality

By Almarse Cooking Class

Mint tea isn't just a drink in Morocco. It's a gesture of welcome, a sign of peace, and an art of balance. The way it's poured, the height, the glass, the first sip—every step has intention.

At Almarse, we don’t just teach you how to brew it—we teach you how to serve it with soul.

And yes, the mint must be fresh. Always.
