How to Prepare

  1. Add gunpowder green tea to a teapot and rinse with hot water.
  2. Add fresh mint leaves and sugar.
  3. Fill teapot with boiling water and steep 5 minutes.
  4. Pour into a glass and back to the pot several times to mix.
  5. Serve in a small glass, optionally garnish with mint.
